## Quick Start

Follow these steps to start your first documentation project in Sreelal Pillai.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Use your email at `https://docs.sreelal-pillai.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create project">
    Click "New Project" and enter a name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a doc page">
    Click "Add Page", choose a title, and start writing. Docs are saved automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
