---
title: I cannot edit my documentation
description: Resolve issues with editing docs.
---

## Symptoms

- Edit button is greyed out
- Can't type or save changes

## Causes

- Read-only permissions
- Network problem or page error

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Ask your project admin to give you editing rights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh or re-login">
    Log out, then log in again. Try editing your page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
