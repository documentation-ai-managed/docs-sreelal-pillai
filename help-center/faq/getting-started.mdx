## Overview

To start, create your first project. Projects let you organize all your documents for each topic or team.

## Steps

1. Log in at `https://docs.sreelal-pillai.com`
2. Click "New Project"
3. Name your project
4. Start adding documentation by clicking "Add Page"

Your docs are auto-saved and easy to organize in folders.
